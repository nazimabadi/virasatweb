  Virasat, meaning heritage, or legacy in Urdu, was founded in 2020 with a mission to preserve, evolve, and represent South Asian arts and culture in Toronto and beyond. As a Toronto-based non-profit organization, we provide a platform for South Asian artists—both emerging and established—to explore complex identities, foster community dialogue, and create innovative multidisciplinary art.


  Our work is deeply rooted in the belief that art is a living, evolving expression of culture. We focus on blending traditional South Asian art forms with contemporary narratives, creating a cultural kaleidoscope that reflects the lived experiences of the South Asian diaspora in Canada.
